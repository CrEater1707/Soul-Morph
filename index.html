Soul Morph — is a roleplay game in 3D anime style. Travel, fight enemies, craft things, find allies and pets and discover world's secrets. You can choose what you want to do and refuse for things you don't like.

About game

Soul Morph is a project created with love to the genre. There'll be 10 characters, each recieves unique ability and path. You are able to choose the sequence of path completion. So you won't miss anything! The player explores a world full of dangers and secrets, finds allies and fights enemies while leveling up the hero.
