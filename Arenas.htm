Multiplayer arenas

Here is the important information about multiplayer arenas in Soul Morph. To fight there you have to spend arena tickets which can be collected on the map or earned by completing quests. When you fight there you will either win or lose. You'll recieve rewards for winning (like resources for crafting armor, which can't be collected in singleplayer games). But if you lose you won't lose anything exept that ticket you spent.

About usual arenas:

When you earn your first arena ticket you'll recieve a message about it and the arena will unlock. The arena is near the starting location of each spawnpoint of each story.

As soon as you enter the arena, you'll have to choose a set of 3 chatacters for fight

There'll be various contestants' groups (like mobile, console, computer, etc.). You'll be able to participate in mixed type fights.(The system will identify your device type (if unknown, you will be moved to "other devices" group (for example if you play on microwave)). But mobile players can also mess with the computer players by joining the tournament. This can't be done reversed because the computer players have better fps. And mobile gamers can try to mess with high level fps players.

About Grand Tournaments:

At the end of each year there'll be Grand Tournaments. 64 best players will qualify for their type of tournament. You also can qualify for a Grand Tournament of your device type. The "best" players are calculated by Win/Lose division (to avoid P2W). Minimal win amount is 100 (if at least 64 players of each group achieve it). After the first Grand Tournament all the qualified (even the ones on 64th places) will recieve big rewards for qualifying and for each win. Of course the rewards will be bigger than usual arena rewards the prizers will recieve huge awards including winner badges. 16 best players of each tournament will qualify for ultimate mixed tournament.

If you qualified to the Grand Tournament there'll be an appointment for the game. If you have another appointment, you can ask me if the game could be played a bit earlier or later. 5 minutes before the game starts, you'll recieve a message about the start if you're aren't in the game yet. If you're in the game, you'll be automatically sent to the waiting room. As soon as the game starts, you'll be teleported to the Grand arena.

Then:

  • The character choice will appear as in usual arena fights.
  • But the difference will be that you'll be able to change the equipment for a minute.
  • After the fight, depended on the result, you'll get a reward.
  • The winner gets an appointment for a new game.
